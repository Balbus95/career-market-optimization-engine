# Career Market & Recruiting Reference Data (2025-2030 Edition)

## A. Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) 2025/2026 Specs
*   **Standard Status:** 99% of Fortune 500 and 75%+ of all recruiters use ATS.
*   **Filter Rate:** ~75% of resumes are filtered out before human review.
*   **The "Anti-Aesthetic" Mandate:** Minimalist functional design wins over complex decoration.
*   **Parsing Rules:**
    *   **Strict Single Column:** Multi-column layouts scramble reading order for 88% of parsers.
    *   **No Tables/Graphics:** Icons, skill bars, and tables are often invisible to NLP engines.
    *   **Safe Fonts:** Roboto, Arial, Calibri, Verdana (10-12pt body, 14-16pt headings).
    *   **Margins:** 0.5" to 1" on all sides.
    *   **Mobile Layout:** Optimized for vertical scrolling on recruiter mobile apps.
*   **Format Priority (Submission):** 1. `.docx` (Gold Standard for recruiter submission) | 2. `PDF` (Standard Text) | 3. `Markdown` (Source of Truth for AI editing — always maintained internally by the engine, never submitted directly).

## B. Master CV Infrastructure (The "Infinite Repository")
*   **Mandate:** Zero length limit. Comprehensive data points for every role.
*   **Structure per Role:** 
    *   **Context:** Company size, tech stack, budget managed, team size.
    *   **STAR Bank:** 5-10 success stories per role (quantifiable + intangible).
    *   **Keywords:** Every skill used, tools, and methodologies.
    *   **Proof:** Links to commits, reports, or portfolio assets.

## C. STAR & Success Story Frameworks
*   **Formula:** [Action Verb] + [Context/Problem] + [Action Taken] + [Result/Metric].
*   **2026 Technical Examples:** 
    *   **Sales/Marketing:** "Launched targeted campaign (A) for underperforming product line (S), generating $500k revenue in Q4 and increasing conversion rates by 27% (R)."
    *   **IT/Development:** "Reduced transaction failure rate from 3% to 0.2% (R) through API refactoring and automated unit testing (A), saving $250k annually (R)."
    *   **Project Management:** "Led team of 8 engineers (A) during corporate restructuring (S), delivering critical project 15 days ahead of schedule and reducing costs by 12% (R)."
*   **2026 "Intangible" Impact Examples:** 
    *   **Client Management:** "Remediated struggling client relationship (S) by implementing proactive account management (A), resulting in 100% account retention and $50k upsell (R)."
    *   **DEI Leadership:** "Spearheaded DEI hiring initiative (S) by refactoring sourcing criteria (A), achieving a 20% increase in diverse candidate pipeline (R)."

### 2. Value-to-Company (V2C) Calculation
*   **Formula:** $V2C = (Generated Revenue + Cost Savings) \times [(Complexity \times 0.5) + (Scarcity \times 0.5)]$
*   **Scarcity scale (1-5):** 1 = Abundant skill, 5 = Unicorn/rare specialist.
*   **Estimation Strategy:** 
    *   **Conservative Baseline:** Use the 25th-50th percentile of industry benchmarks for qualifications alone.
    *   **Value Multiplier:** Add 5-10% for proven STAR achievements that exceed role expectations (e.g., $250k saved).
    *   **Remote Adjustments:** Factor in technology stipends vs. local cost-of-living (CoL) adjustments.

### 3. Realistic Appraisal Rules
*   **Objectivity:** Base estimates ONLY on proven certifications, years of specialized experience, and JD requirements.
*   **Non-Overestimation:** Explicitly discount "soft" claims that lack a STAR framework or measurable metric.
*   **Frequency Factor:** If a skill is rare (Freq < 2 in market), apply a 15% "Scarcity Premium" to the base rate.
