# Career Market & Recruiting Reference Data (2025-2030 Edition)

## A. Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) 2026 Specs
* **Standard Status:** 99% of Fortune 500 and 75%+ of all recruiters use ATS.
* **Filter Rate:** ~75% of resumes are filtered out before human review.
* **The "Anti-Aesthetic" Mandate:**
    * **Strict Single Column:** Multi-column layouts scramble the reading order for 88% of parsers.
    * **No Tables/Graphics:** Icons, skill bars, and tables are often invisible or corrupt data extraction.
    * **2026 Safe Fonts:** Lora, Roboto, Calibri, Verdana (10-12pt body, 14-16pt headings).
    * **Personalization:** Ensure the "Human Voice" is present in the summary to avoid being flagged as "AI-Spam."
    * **Mobile Layout:** Optimized for vertical scrolling on recruiter mobile apps.

## B. STAR & Success Story Frameworks
* **Formula:** [Action Verb] + [Context/Problem] + [Action Taken] + [Result/Metric].
* **2026 "Intangible" Impact Examples:** 
    * "Remediated struggling client relationship (S) by implementing proactive account management (A), resulting in 100% account retention and $50k upsell (R)."
    * "Spearheaded DEI hiring initiative (S) by refactoring sourcing criteria (A), achieving a 20% increase in diverse candidate pipeline (R)."

## C. LinkedIn Optimization 2.0 (2025-2030)
* **Headline:** [Exact Target Title] | [Key Accomplishment/ROI] | [3-4 Core Hard Skills].
* **UVP Hook:** The first 275 characters must define your Unique Value Proposition (UVP) in first person.
* **Engagement Hack:** Use the "Featured" section for PDF case studies (Problem -> Solution -> Result) to boost credibility.
